My Interests

My top 3 interests are skiing, traveling, and music.

Skiing

I have been skiing with my Dad since I was 2 years old. It's always been a fun thing we've done together. I briefly skiied competitively, but was in a bad accident. Since then, I haven't raced again, but I've never missed a season.

Traveling

I have been very fortunate to have had amazing travel opportunies and experiences. My family and I love to travel and explore different countries and cultures.

I have never viewed traveling as a "vacation," because they usually aren't relaxing trips. My Dad makes sure we wake up early and make the most of our time in new places.

Music

I love listening to music. My favorite genre is hip-hop, but I really love all types of music. After taking a music course at Babson, I learned how limiting genres are, and how much great music there is of all genres.
